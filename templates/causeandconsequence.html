Bike Ride to Stonewall

Cause and Consequence

This event was caused because my friends and I wanted to do something somewhat big after we finished all our assessments of our grade 10 year. We all loved biking so we were planning on a biking trip to go to somewhere. As a joke, I mentioned that we should go to Stonewall and surprisingly my friend took it seriously and we ended up all agreeing to go. My consequence I faced on my life after this event was that I missed going to a restaurant that my parents and another family planned on to get together. The food would have been good too, which I was looking forward to when my friends and I got back. However, my friends and I got back at 11pm because we wanted to stay at Stonewall for a bit longer, so I missed the outing.
