How do living things aquire energy?! !Humans/Animals aquire energy through food, oxygen and water and through cellular respiration CO2, H2O, and ATP (adenosine triphosphate). Animals get energy from the food which is glucose (sugar), and carbohydrates are large chains of glucose. Animals cannot get energy from the sun directly. Plants on the otherhand get energy from photosynthesis by obtaining sunlight and water.! !
